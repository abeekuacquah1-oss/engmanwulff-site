Our Studio

Built on craft,
rooted in Accra.

Engman Wulff began with a simple belief: that the stories worth telling deserve to be told with care. We are a small studio of photographers, filmmakers, and producers working across Ghana and the wider region.

Every assignment — whether a documentary following one family for months, or a single afternoon on a portrait set — is approached with the same patience and discipline. We shoot what's real, and we shoot it well.
